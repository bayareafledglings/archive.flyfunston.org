Thomas Low
Launching my Wills Wing 160 Duck at Funston. 1983 or 84. Right downtube has my vario, left a G meter. Check out the cliff edge stabilization effort. Harness mains were 1/8" stainless steel cables. The flight suit was a gift from Rob Kells. Before there were PG's I was the most color coordinated pilot around.
