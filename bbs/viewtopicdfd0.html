Yesterday a strong sheer cloud appeared over Westlake. Classic conditions - light winds, warm day, then turns strong NW about 2:30. Today looks to do the same thing - if you get off early enough you may beat the too strong winds which built up quickly yesterday. Cloud extended to Devils Slide - potential for Half Moon Bay.
Not for the timid!
