Funston Parking Lot and New Federal Law

by Dan Brown » Fri Apr 01, 2005 2:06 pm

The Club is in violation of the recently passed Equal Opportunity for Senior Americans Act (EOSAA) for failing to reserve parking spaces at the west end of the parking lot for pilots over 62. EOSAA requires equal access on all federal recreation sites.

The law makes officers of organizations violating the law subject to fines of up to $1,000.00 day.
