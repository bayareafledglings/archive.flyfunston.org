Sonoma Wings Chute Repack Clinic

Posted: Wed Feb 28, 2018 7:25 pm
by GeorgiK
Hi, All
Come and shake the dust off your chute at the annual SW chute repack clinic and get ready for the new fly season. Sunday March 11, 10 am till we are done. Bring something for the potluck, but don`t eat much till after we spin you up for your deployment exercise.
The clinic is free to all, but we greatly appreciate and encourage you to become a member of Sonoma Wings when you come .Only $25 to become a member of this esteemed organization. Standard rubber bands recommended by the rigging shops - (2"x3/8"x0.060" in size) will be provided free of charge by Sonoma Wings. If you need different size(s) please bring them on.
See you there.


Venue: Sonoma Sky Park
21870 8th St E, Sonoma, CA 95476
March 11, @ 10 am
http://www.sonomaskypark.com/
