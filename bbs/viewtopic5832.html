December 5, 2019 Fellow Feathers' Meeting Minutes

by RonB » Thu Dec 05, 2019 9:25 pm

Attendees;
  • Rob Racer (Prez)
  • Brad Martin (Safety)
  • John Simpson (Clubhouse)
  • Ron Barry (secretary)
  • Steve Rodrigues (treasurer)
  • Diver Dave
  • Isaiah Clapp

Open Discussion;
  • 10am Saturday, please be present for an evaluation of our drainage issues and possible attempt to rectify.
  • Warnings for the 25-foot rule, this year, have been rampant.
  • Motion to change the rule from 25' clearance (difficult to judge by eyeball)
    to one wingspan making it much easier to relate to and understand.
    Motion Carried.

Election for 2020.
  • Ron Barry - Secretary
  • Ron Barry - Vice President
  • Steve Rodrigues - Treasurer
  • George Artz - Tech (Ron will serve as assistant/backup, if George will provide intro.)
  • Isaiah Clapp - President
  • Rob Racer - Clubhouse Manager
  • Brad Martin - Safety Officer

Rob Racer (President's Report)
  • Visited fort today. Clubhouse dry.
  • We need to do a dig day to get the drainage issues under control. The last
    major rainstorm eroded a chunk off of launch. Discussion as to how we can
    alter the current drainage issues.

Treasurer's Report (Steve Rodrigues)
  • $12k + 3282.59 liquid assets. No major expenses until November, 2020 GGNRA
    payment.
  • Clubhouse full. 10-11 spaces are duplicates (people who have 2.) THey're
    allowed with the understanding that they'll give up a spot for new residents.
  • Buried second power cable. (First one was a test. Seemed to work, so he did
    the second one.)
  • New anememeter cable has been run. Vane adjusted. Windtalker working again.
  • George has been trying to stabilize the tech. Steve put him in touch with
    the guy who wrote most of our stuff.
