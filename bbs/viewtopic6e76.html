by Steve Rodrigues » Sat Mar 16, 2013 1:19 pm

Hi Diev,
Besides a power outage that just crashed some of our gear, we also have anemometer issues. Here is an email I just sent to the Board:

"The club has two working anemometers on the tower: One is for the dial-up wind talker, the other is for the web site. I adjusted the one that feeds the web site, which is the lowest unit on the tower.

The old anemometer I took down (and someone saw in the garbage) was the old web site anemometer that had gone bad and was replaced but was never taken down.

Our dial-up anemometer is the middle unit on the tower. This is the one that we need to replace. Like Chuck said, we need to find the correct device that will function with the prehistoric but so-far amazingly durable Litek unit.

Note: We do *NOT* own the anemometer and weather vane on the very tip of the tower. That very top unit belongs to the Bay Area Air Quality Management District who is the owner of the tower and gives us permission to mount our units on it."
