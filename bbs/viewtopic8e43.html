Free H-2 Tandem flights

Posted: Sun Mar 22, 2009 8:24 am

I want to keep our H-2's motivated and on a path to becoming a better pilot so I'll be happy to take any H-2's for a free tandem orientation flight at Fort Funston. You can learn the best places to make turns, how to set up landing approaches, and get a real feel for the site. I don't always have my Tandem Falcon with me, so if the forecast is for 15-25 mph, SW to W, drop me an email so we can go get some! Right now I'm only available on weekends.
