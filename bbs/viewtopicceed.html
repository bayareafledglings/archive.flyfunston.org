Aerotow Clinic This Weekend

Posted: Thu Feb 17, 2005 11:58 am
by diev
Spaces available in the Aerotow Clinic this weekend.

We have two spaces available in the Saturday clinic and if we get
enough interest in a Sunday clinic that will be available.

Cost of the Aerotow Clinic is $275. You will receive a full ground
school a minimum of 5 tows to 2000' and helpful critiques of every
flight. All pilots will also be trained in the art of tow assistant
to better aid there fellow pilots. Those pilots who successfully
finishing the course will be eligible for the AT signoff from the
USHGA.

Come learn to tow with the pros at the New Jerusalem Airport in Tracy.
Contact Ken Brown for details.
