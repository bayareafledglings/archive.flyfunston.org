July 2013 Fellow Feathers Meeting Minutes

Posted: Mon Sep 23, 2013 4:27 pm
by tom white
In attendance:
Tom White, Rob Johnson, Tracey Story, David Kiesling, Patrick Pannese,Tom Low, Gene Foley, Rafael Lavin, Tom Rust, John Simpson, Chuck Kranz, Ken Martin, Rick Hawkins, Steve Rodrigues, Brian Porter, Janica Lee, Diev Hart, Van Pelham, Charlie Nelson, Mark Lilledal, Harry, Chris Carillo

Gene Foley called for more vintage photos of HG at Funston, to scan into an archive.
Tracey reported that the spot landing comp brought in $500.
Volunteers are sought to help manage upcoming air races. H2's who want to fly Funston - volunteer!
Steve reported that stickers are now in lock box. Some costs were involved, members present passed a motion to pay for the costs from maintenance fund.
Mark L reported that the logs piled at top of sand trail are a hazard.
Tom R proposed a amendment requiring all suspensions to be voted on by the entire membership, and removing the responsibility from the executive committee. Motion seconded, voted. For - 2, against - 14.
Rob J proposed a vintage HG fly in and BBQ. Rob will clean and epoxy coat the floor of the clubhouse soon.
Steve reported that the H2 soaring program is in progress.
Meeting adjourned.
