MLSR Coyote Howl (May 19th & 20th)

Posted: Wed May 02, 2018 7:43 pm
by crvalley
The Mother Lode Sky Riders (MLSR) Coyote Howl Fly-In is May 19th & 20th, 2018 at Lake McClure!

Activities include Speed Gliding Saturday morning, then Pylon Course, Horseshoe Dash, and Spot Landings in the afternoon - or, just free fly.

BBQ and "Bragging Rights" Award Ceremony Saturday evening - bring your glider and have some fun!

H2's welcome with pre-arrangments made with a Club Officer.

Lake McClure campgrounds are open and the lake is full!

Camping reservations via MID - MERCED IRRIGATION DISTRICT WATER & POWER.

Direct: 209-354-2954/1-855-800-2267 (CAMP), or http://www.lakemcclure.com/

Thanks for your support!
