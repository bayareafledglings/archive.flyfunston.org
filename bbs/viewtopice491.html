Resigning from The Board of Directors.

Posted: Tue Aug 03, 2021 9:15 am
by RonB
I joined The Fort Funston Board of Directors because I really thought I could make a difference. I hoped to spend some time in minor roles, learn the environment, then serve in a greater capacity over time. I intended to be a present and helpful part of this organization for as long as I was flying.

I can no longer, in good conscience, remain. My resignation is effective immediately. I take this action of my own volition to resolve an otherwise unavoidable, recurrent ethical dilemma. I hope to continue flying for as long as I can, though recent events leave me in no hurry to return to the site.

Please do not contact me about the circumstances. I am leaving for ethical reasons and believe it would be a breach of ethics to discuss them.

Ron
