December Fellow Feathers meeting

Posted: Tue Nov 11, 2008 11:58 pm
by Steve Rodrigues
The next FF meeting will be on Tuesday December 9th at 7:30 PM.
This is the second Tuesday of the month, as always.

This will be an important meeting where new club officers will be elected for 2009.

There will a number of positions available on the Executive Committee, so if you have the time to contribute to our club, please come to the meeting and offer your services.

Even if you don't want to hold a position yourself, please come and cast your vote for the people you think will do the best job.

Free pizza for everyone who votes! (and even those who don't)
