It is not a Shear!

Posted: Thu Feb 04, 2021 12:39 pm
by Steve Rodrigues
.
Fort Funston is famed for its near mythical “Shear”, a weather anomaly that we all yearn to encounter. But calling it a "shear" is incorrect and leads to a misunderstanding of the conditions and how to best fly in them.

We should take note from the sail plane pilots who more accurately call it what it is: Convergence!

For more info and insight for making the most of a convergence: http://soaringcafe.com/2012/07/weather- ... eze-front/
