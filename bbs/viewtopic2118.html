March 1st 2018 Fellow Feather’s Meeting Minutes

In Attendance: Rob Johnson, Chris Valley, Barton Davidson, Walter Whiteside, Brian Porter, Randee Azzar.

Guests / New Members: No guests or new members.

Great Flights: CRV and JT went to the beach last weekend and had a great hike back up to launch. Good flights at Mission over the holiday weekend: Wayne Michelsen flew to Casa de Fruita and Ben Dunn flew to Tres Pinos. Abashek flew from Mission to Morgan Hill.

President’s Report (Rob Johnson): Grass in launch area is maintained. New porta-potties should be installed in the not so distant future. Walkway to the deck reconstruction is underway by the Park.

Vice President’s Report (Wayne Michelsen): Not present. Nothing to report.

Secretary’s Report (Chris Valley): February minutes posted.

Treasurer’s Report (Steve Rodrigues): Not present. Current budget is solid at $13,509.

Clubhouse Officer’s Report (Rob Johnson): Clubhouse is still standing. 40% have paid their dues.

Safety Officer’s Report (John Simpson): Not present, but we need to emphasize the importance of safety for the upcoming flying season. No incidents to report.

Tech Officer’s Report (Chuck Kranz): Not present, but everything is up and running fine.

Administrator Reports:

~ Tandem (Urs Kellenberger): Not present. Nothing to report.
~ Training Bowl (John Simpson): Not present. Nothing to report.

GGNRA Liaison Report (Steve Rodrigues): Not present, but we are in good standing with the GGNRA. New garbage cans installed in the parking lot area.

Old Business: No old business.

New Business: No new business.

Meeting adjourned at 8:00 PM.
