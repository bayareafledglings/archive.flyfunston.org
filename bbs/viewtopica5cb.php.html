Memorial BBQ and Air Races! Saturday, August 15

by Steve Rodrigues » Sat Jul 18, 2009 12:26 am

The annual Dan Murphy Memorial BBQ will be held on Saturday, August 15. A re-birth of the Funston Air Races is expected to take place the same day!
Everyone is invited to come fly and hang out. The BBQ will be fired up in the afternoon and the timed flights to Westlake will start whenever it becomes soarable. Anyone interested in helping with the BBQ (shopping, meat flipping, etc) should contact Rafael Lavin. Those who would like to help with the Air Races (timing, record keeping, etc) should contact Steve Rodrigues. Rumor has it that certain Tandem Instructors might even be offering free flights!
See you there!

FF Air Race

by Urs » Mon Aug 17, 2009 9:56 am

Thanks to all the volunteers, participants and spectators that made this a fun race and a great BBQ.

Seeing 8 gliders on the beach at one time showed the go for it spirit that prevailed during the race.

I've been to 4 other air races and ended up on the beach each time, twice a t Torrey Pines, twice at the Marina Steeple Chase (middle of the course). I kinda expected to do so during this race as well.

I didn't free fly before the race so I didn't know how light the winds were until I returned to the Fort and let the bar out. Had I free flown beforehand, I may not have ventured to Westlake.

Thanks again to all!
