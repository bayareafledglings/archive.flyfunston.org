Where to send your FF membership applications

Posted: Mon Feb 13, 2017 2:37 pm
by Steve Rodrigues
.
This posting is to clear up any confusion over where to send your Fellow Feathers/Fort Funston membership and sticker applications. Please remember that "Clubhouse Member" applications are for pilots who want to keep their glider in the clubhouse. All other pilots are "Non-Clubhouse" members!

What to include:
-Completed membership application. Clubhouse applications must also include a signed Clubhouse Waiver.
-Annual dues and/or donation (thank you!).
-A copy of your USHPA card.
-A self addressed stamped envelope.

Mail Clubhouse applications to:
Rob Johnson
PO Box 1176
Mountain View, CA 94042

Mail Non-Clubhouse applications to:
Fellow Feathers
530 Lawrence Expy
Suite 400
Sunnyvale, CA 94085

Note: New H-3's and H-4's who have never flown at Fort Funston must have a site intro before getting their sticker and are not eligible for sticker-by-mail.

Links to membership applications and other info can be found here: http://flyfunston.org/sticker.php.html
