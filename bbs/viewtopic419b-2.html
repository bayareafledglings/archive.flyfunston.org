Dog management plan meetings!

by Steve Rodrigues » Sat Oct 26, 2013 10:50 am

There are 3 more public meetings where you can comment on the proposed dog management plan.

The public comment period for the Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement (SEIS) has been extended by 5 weeks, from December 4, 2013 to January 11, 2014, due both to the recent 16-day government shutdown and requests from stakeholders.

It is not necessary for individuals, organizations, and agencies that have already commented to do so again, unless they wish to submit a new comment. CDs and a limited number of hard copies of the SEIS are available and can be requested at 415-561-4728. The SEIS is available online at: http://parkplanning.nps.gov/dogplan

The public meetings will occur as previously scheduled:

November 2, 2013
11:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Fort Mason Center - Building D, Fleet Room
Marina Boulevard at Buchanan
San Francisco, California

November 4, 2013
4:30 PM - 8:30 PM
Farallone View Elementary School
1100 Le Conte Avenue
Montara, California

November 6, 2013
4:00 PM - 8:00 PM
Tamalpais High School - Ruby Scott Gym
700 Miller Avenue
Mill Valley, California
USHPA # 30605
