by crvalley » Wed Jun 11, 2008 6:31 am

Well, the pizza was good and Dingo Dave even brought some appetizers that were pretty tasty, too...

I wiped off the glass portal for the web cam, but there is an inner blotch somewhere that still shows up...you can see the Fort better regardless.

Gwenhael will post the minutes...otherwise the primary discussion was whether or not to allow T1's to fly Funston. It was decided to continue the discussion for our next meeting. One point made was that Funston is not necessarily the place for training T1's, yet could be of benefit under the supervision of a T2 and/or T3...more later.

Another topic was providing really good site introductions to new H3's flying our site. Take the time to go over all the nuances of flying Funston. Have the pilot shoot a few landings. Make sure the pilot knows ridge soaring rules. If they aren't flying well, there is nothing wrong with sending them back to their instructor for more help.

Fly safe!
