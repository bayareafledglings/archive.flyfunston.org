MLSR/McClure Coyote Howl May 19-22

by Daniel Pifko » Mon Apr 11, 2005 1:31 pm

This from the Mother Lode Sky Riders:


May 19-22: The 2005 Coyote Howl Fly-In and Cloudbuster Hootenanny at Lake McClure, California. Meet directed by USHGA Vice-President Lisa Tate. Free-flying and XC Thursday & Friday, May 19-20, organized meet Saturday & Sunday, May 21-22. $20 pilot participation fee includes raffle and event prizes. The flying is great at that time of the year, and the partying should live up to our normal high standards. Contact Shannon Raby for meet information: getusum@inreach.com or (209) 878-0249.

Saturday night hootenanny, $25 for dinner and show by Doodoo Wah, Sourdough Slim, and The Saddle Pals, advance ticket purchase required. Fast paced, hilariously entertaining show will make you hoot and howl and holler, maybe even slap your thighs! Contact Reklai Salazar for entertainment tickets at reklai@hotmail.com or (916) 799-5231.

Great flying Thursday through Sunday, butt-kicking entertainment Saturday night, and friendly locals will make this a memorable event and one you won’t want to miss.

Additional event information at http://www.mlsr.net/2005coyotehowl.htm


HERE’S WHAT YOU’VE GOTTA DO:

RSVP is appreciated but not required for the Fly-in, or the Mexican dinner Friday nite, or the breakfast Sunday morning; just showing up is OK too.

But if you want to get in on the Hootenanny, because the room is so small and we expect to have more folks wanting in than we have room for, we need to have the concert tickets prepaid. We can hold tickets for will-call at the door, but only if they've been prepaid. Sorry we've gotta be so strict, but we're dealing with real live musician/entertainers, and this is gonna be show to remember. The room holds about 100, we have about 30 tickets left as of 4-10-05.

You can mail payment, $25.00 per person covers the Saturday nite meal and Hootenanny, to me:
Accent Corp
8250 Ferguson Ave
Sacramento, CA 95828

If you'd rather use a credit card via Paypal to my email address, send $26.05 for 1, $51.80 for 2, or $103.30 for 4.

If you include your return address, I'll mail your tickets out to you, otherwise we'll hold at the door for will-call. The tickets themselves are collector's items, you'll love 'em.


COYOTE HOWL FLY-IN CALENDAR:

THURSDAY, MAY 19
All Day Free flying, XC, and fun

FRIDAY, MAY 20
All Day Free flying, XC, and fun
4:00 pm Party in the LZ, bring your musical instruments
5:00 pm Mexican dinner in LZ, $5

SATURDAY, MAY 21
8:30 am Speed Gliding
10:30 am Pylon Run, Duration, XC, Spot Landing tasks as called by Meet Director
6:30 pm Dinner in the Magnolia Saloon, Coulterville (advance purchase required)
7:30 pm Cloudbuster Hootenanny above the Magnolia Saloon (advance purchase required)

SUNDAY, MAY 22
8:00 am Breakfast by VFW served until 10:00
8:30 am Speed Gliding
10:00 am Awards and Raffle
11:00 am Free flying, XC, and fun
