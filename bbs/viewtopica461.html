2009 Coyote Howl at McClure

Posted: Wed Mar 18, 2009 8:31 pm
by crvalley
It is that time of year again...the time when we enjoy morning speed gliding and then put our gliders up on a wingtip for daytime flying...yes...it's time for...

2009 Coyote Howl at McClure

This year's Coyote Howl will be held May 16th and 17th...

The speed gliding competition will be held both days at 9:00 am. There will be a pilot's meeting in the LZ at 10:30 am on Saturday.

Will Brian Foster defend his 1st place speed gliding trophy?

This is a one day competition, so the award ceremony will be held on Sunday at approximately 11:00 a.m. There will be trophies for the pylon course, the horseshoe dash, cross country, spot landing, and of course bragging rights for altitude.

The fee to enter is $30 and this includes the barbecue and drinks. This is a necessary fund raiser for the Mother Lode Sky Riders and we thank you for your support.

Bring the family, have some fun...hook into your glider and...GET U SUM!

Hope to see you there!
