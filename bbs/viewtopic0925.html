FF Sticker Must be Visible on Helmet

Reminder to all Funston pilots: Our GGNRA Special Use Permit, Section G, sub-section 4, paragraph b states: “Current FF permit stickers are required and must be visible on pilot helmets.”

Due to recent events, GGNRA Rangers and U.S. Park Police will be actively checking for helmet stickers. Please make sure that your helmet sticker is current and visible or you will be subject to citation and suspension of flying privileges.

Thank you for helping the authorities protect our flying site!
