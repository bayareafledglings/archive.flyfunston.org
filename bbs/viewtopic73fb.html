Fellow Feathers meeting on Tuesday, January 8th at 7:30 PM

Posted: Mon Jan 07, 2008 8:47 am
by Steve Rodrigues
The next Fellow Feathers meeting will be on Tuesday, January 8th at 7:30 PM.

One topic for discussion will be changing the Fellow Feathers fiscal year to a period of time that would better relate our income to our expenses. The FF bylaws currently state our fiscal year runs from July 1 through June 30. Membership and sticker donations begin around December and taper off by September, so this time period splits the income in a weird way. Our treasurer proposes that a fiscal year running from October 1 through September 30 would work better. We need to vote on this at two consecutive meetings to change the bylaws. The first vote will be at the upcoming meeting.

Another topic is the idea of raising the requirements for H-3 pilots to fly Westlake unsupervised.

We will also share free pizza, so brave the weather and come on down!!!
