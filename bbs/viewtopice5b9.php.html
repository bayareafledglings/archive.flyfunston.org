New wind meter

Posted: Fri Feb 02, 2007 11:02 pm
by Daniel Pifko
There is now a new weather station on top of the clubhouse. It is feeding wind data to our web page so we have a more reliable source that also happens to be more representative of what's going on at the cliff. It is a completely separate system than the wind talker, since that bunny just goes and goes and I don't want to fool with it.

Just for now, I've put some basic charts up on the webcam page - http://flyfunston.org/newwebcam . The wind pointer graphic is a recent average (5 or 10 minutes, I think) and the blue number is the speed. The red number is the max speed, though I'm not sure yet over what period. As you can tell, I'm still working out some of the details but I wanted to get this puppy online at least.

We estimated due West so the readings may be a little bit off.

You can see the full reports at http://flyfunston.org/img and http://flyfunston.org/img/Current.htm .

We are also pumping the weather into the Weather Underground. You can see it there at http://www.wunderground.com/weatherstat ... KCASANFR66 .

Have fun,

Daniel

http://www.iwindsurf.com/windandwhere.i ... iteID=2640
