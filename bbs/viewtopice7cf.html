To: USHPA Chapters and Schools
From: US Hang Gliding & Paragliding Headquarters
Subject: 20th Century Fox & USHPA Partner for RIO Film Release

Hello Chapters and Schools:

USHPA has partnered with 20th Century Fox to roll out the red carpet for the release of RIO, an animated film that features a hang gliding segment in Rio de Janeiro. USHPA is working with 20th Century Fox to harness their far-reaching distribution channels and large audience. In hopes of creating a successful partnership with 20th Century Fox and opening future doors, USHPA is asking chapters and schools to step forward and help capitalize on the marketing campaign. This is a voluntary project organized by USHPA to utilize a nationally focused marketing infrastructure to help promote schools, chapters, clubs, and the sport in general.

The new movie RIO will be shown in almost every theater across the country on April 15th. One of the themes of the movie is flying and gaining confidence in oneself to do so. One scene—learning to fly at the Cristo Launch at Rio de Janeiro, Brazil (thus, the name RIO)—is specifically about hang gliding. We are thrilled that 20th Century Fox is working with us, using their brand to strengthen our brand—hang gliding! Watch the scene here or go to our YouTube Account. http://www.youtube.com/user/USHangGlidingParagli

You already participate in the dream of flying. Let's share our passion with the public! Did you know it takes up to 4-5 exposures of a product (seeing a flier, looking at a website, hearing about it from a friend, seeing a film with it, hearing about it on the radio, etc.) before a person will commit to taking part in a particular activity or purchasing a product. TV and radio ads repeat continually, just to make sure you’re listening at least one of the times you hear them. We need your help to be successful.

With that in mind, we've worked a deal with 20th Century Fox - requesting tandem gift certificates and intro lesson giveaways for the following locations: Albuquerque, Atlanta/Chattanooga Region, Denver, Los Angeles, New York, Orlando Salt Lake City, San Francisco, and Virginia Beach/Kitty Hawk Region.

In these markets USHPA will be sponsoring video advertisements in nine theaters for six weeks April 15-May 26th, 2011. This fifteen-second ad was produced by USHPA specifically for the RIO cross- promotional campaign and will run before every movie in each of these nine theaters. We will make a downloadable version available on our Vimeo Account and on our website in the upcoming week if you wish to buy advertising in your region. The theaters were chosen for their proximity to large training areas and the pilot communities willing to donate time to follow up with fly-ins and other volunteer marketing projects. As this is USHPA’s first attempt at using a video advertisement and the cost associated with the endeavor is relatively high, we need to keep the sample size small to be able to successfully measure the efficacy of this push. More information on the national pitch will be found at our website at http://www.flylikeabird.org(COMING SOON).

Kindle the inspiration to experience free flight that people will have when they exit the movie theater. They need your help to take those fledgling steps to make it a reality in their lives. Don’t let them walk away from the movie saying, "Hang gliding looks dangerous!" Let's erase that reaction by giving them positive information and getting them excited about the possibility of living the dream.

In 1911, almost 100 years ago this summer, two brothers at Kitty Hawk, North Carolina, had a dream of soaring that came true. It's as relevant today as it was then. Introducing the dream to would-be pilots is the best gift we (as a group that needs growth) can give our sport and the world.

Rafael (George Lopez voice), a character from RIO tells his little Blue Macaw pals (voiceover by Jessie Eisenberg and Anne Hathaway), "If our featherless friends can do it, how hard can it be?"

Let's go out and show them what free flight is all about! If you're interested in participating and need our help, please don't hesistate to call or email us. We will be calling as many of you as we can.

There's MORE!!!!!!
