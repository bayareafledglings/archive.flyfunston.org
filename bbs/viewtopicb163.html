Sewer repair

Posted: Wed Oct 27, 2010 8:52 pm
by Steve Rodrigues
Received this update from our GGNRA contact:

Daly City/North San Mateo County Sanitation District is planning to begin some repairs and maintenance on their force main pipe and beach outfall structure next week at Fort Funston.

I don't have a specific date, yet, but I think they're going to want to start staging sometime this week in the parking lot and start work Nov. 1. The work may go into the following week.

They will use approx. 5 parking spaces in the parking lot for a staging area and to fence off an area for equipment and materials storage.
