Pilots needed for 16mm short film

Posted: Fri Oct 22, 2004 6:45 pm
by Nick D.
All experience levels are encouraged to inquire.

This short, 16mm documentary film will focus on 2-4 personal stories about those who hang glide: a reflective and inspirational film more suited for PBS rather than MTV.

Pilots with interesting circumstances (eg, a-typical day jobs, near-death experiences, husband/wife tandem teams, etc.) are encouraged to inquire. Pilots with physical disabilities are strongly encouraged.

Though no financial compensation is available, all selected participants will be credited in the film and will receive a free copy on VHS or DVD. The film will be submitted for screening at local, national, and international film festivals.

This film will be shot & produced within the Bay Area.
Shooting Schedule: OCTOBER 30th - NOVEMBER 6th.

If interested, please contact local filmmaker, Nick Davila at (650)799-2411 or ndavila@stanford.edu.
