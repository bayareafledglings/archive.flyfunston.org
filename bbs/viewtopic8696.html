Ocean current antennas: see them on May 30; discuss June 13

Posted: Wed May 24, 2006 6:15 pm
by Daniel Pifko
Please see the discussion about the Ocean Currents Monitoring project in this bulletin board thread: http://flyfunston.org/bbs/viewtopic.php.html?t=309

On May 30, the scientists will be test-installing the antennas in the spots agreed upon by Henry, Steve, Chester and Daniel. They will go up around noon and come down around 5 pm.

Please come by Funston during that time to see what they look like and whether you feel they pose a safety hazard.

We will discuss this at the Fellow Feathers meeting June 13th, starting at 8:00 pm. Regan Long, from the project, will be there to present the topic and answer any questions people have. When she is finished we'll carry on the discussion amongst the club members to determine if all our concerns have been met and if sufficient steps have been taken to ensure safe flying at Funston.

Please come join us for the club meeting.

Thanks,

Daniel
