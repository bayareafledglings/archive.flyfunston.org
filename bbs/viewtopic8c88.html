Hat Creek Porta Potties

Posted: Wed Apr 06, 2016 9:55 am
by strawfly
Both Porta Potties were vandalized at Hat Creek in March and were filled with construction foam

The HCPA have attempted to remove the construction foam from the PP,s at Hat Creek but have found it very difficult to impossible.
http://www.hanggliding.org/viewtopic.ph ... 04&start=0

We are looking at buying replacement PP,s or just replacement tanks for them if they are available. This will take some funds and we have some T shirts left over from last year that we would like to sell off at a good price to help us with the additional expense.

We are selling these shirts or $10.00 inc s&h to try and raise some money to get our Porta Potties replaced as soon as possible for the upcoming season.

You can get your shirts here http://hatcreekrim.com/donations/

Please make a trip to Hat Creek and enjoy the great air and pilots.


Roger Jackson
