At some point we're going to have to get back to the mundane topics involved in operating Fort Funston, so I'll start off with something super straightforward to ease our way back in.

We have too many piles of chips, and the "chips" have so much greenery in them that they end up molding, dusting and eventually leaving a slimey later on our gliders.

IF YOU SEE A DUMP TRUCK dropping more chips on launch, please let someone on the Funston exec know the company name and, if possible, phone number. You can also ask the truck driver to hold off for a month and, even then, to only give us brown bits - no green.

Thanks,

Daniel
