Annual Andy Jackson Memorial Fly-In - September 11, 2010

Our annual end-of-summer bash to celebrate the memory of Andy Jackson,
who moved his family to an unimproved dirt lot in the 1970s to ensure
that pilots had a legal place to land when flying from Crestline. The
existence of Pinecrest Airpark led directly to our ability to negotiate
with the State of California to secure the current AJ Airpark when
their expansion of the Devil Canyon hydroelectric plant shut Pinecrest
down. We lost Andy to cancer in 1985.

Expect the usual great flying, camaraderie, food, and fun contests.

We will be having a bomb drop contest and spot landing contest and we
will be having barbecue steaks and chicken for dinner.

Juanita Jackson is having special shirts made for the event - see:
http://www.crestlinesoaring.org/ajmemorial2010shirts
