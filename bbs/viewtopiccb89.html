NEW BUSINESS

Nick Davila – film maker in Stanford’s graduate film program
• Making a short film about a pilot’s story. Chose Harold Johnson as the subject.
• B&W film. Portrait of Harold. Pursuing HG later in life.
• He will allow us to put the movie on our web site.
• Also will be formatted for DVD and VHS
• Looking for a grant from USHGF; USHGF in favor, but they are looking for local support too.
• In return, he’ll give us a credit in the film, invite us to the screening, give us copies, provide downloadable version for flyfunston.org
• Screening is first or second week of December – he’ll email us We could use it for promotional purposes, though not for selling.
• Motion: $400 grant, in return for permission to put the film online, credit in the film and a pointer for anyone who wants to learn how to fly to www.USHGA.org. All but Raffy and Larry in favor. Motion passed.
