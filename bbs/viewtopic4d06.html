Meeting Minutes June 9, 2009

Posted: Tue Sep 22, 2009 8:37 pm
by Steve Rodrigues
Meeting June 9, 2009

Tom White, Urs Kellenberger, Eric Hinrichs, Steve Rodrigues, Enzo Fatica, Tom Jensen, Jose Pruneda, Anamarja Plavec, Armand Petersen, David Royer, Chris Valley, Dirk Morris, Damien Dykman, Jimmie Fulton, Chuck Kranz, Larry Carpenter, Mark Lilledhal, Daniel Pifko, Dan Brown, Ted Lipke, Chester Browne, Eric Mies, Patrick Aiken, Raphael Lavin, Charlie Nelson

Steve R discusses new signs for LZ, passes around dog sign draft.

More sticker packages made

Can Berkeley club have one key and share a space with multiple members? FF members agree no, Berkeley club can’t share space. Rules don’t allow it

SR announces annual Dan Murphy BBQ and purgatory glider auction for August 15.

Treasurers report will be given next month.

SR says revisions to tandem program are coming.

Tom Jensen, training bowl director gave report. Launches are better now, some new pilots now training in the bowl, limited soaring has been working. Talked about requiring at least H-1. Discussed bowl waivers. Mark L. submitted waiver

SR announces attorney Gene Takagi has written new bylaws for club.

Dan Brown proposes $200- donation to Youth Leadership Fund in the name of deceased GGNRA superintendent Brian O’Neill. In favor-18, opposed-0

Dan B says FF incorporation process muddled. Dispute between Dan and Steve over bylaws and incorporation process. Urs makes motion to have a committee review proposed bylaws and recommend one set. In favor-11, opposed-7

Bylaws Review Committee will be: Martin Beresford, Dan Brown, Steve R, Chris Valley, Tom Jensen.
