Helicopter re-scheduled to Friday

Posted: Fri Oct 09, 2009 3:12 pm
by Steve Rodrigues
Due to the poor weather forecast for Tuesday, the helicopter flight at Fort Funston is being re-scheduled to Friday, October 9.

The entire west side of the parking lot will be closed off to the public and there will be NO hang gliding while the chopper is in the area.

They will try and start in the morning and expect to work 1/2 day, but bad weather could delay the start time and cause them to work later.
All times are UTC - 8 hours [ DST ]
