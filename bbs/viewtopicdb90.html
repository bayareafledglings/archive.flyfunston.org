Just a reminder, the Fellow Feathers executive board elections for 2007 will take place at the upcoming December 12 meeting.

Meeting time and election slate

Posted: Thu Dec 07, 2006 2:55 pm
by Daniel Pifko
The meeting next Tuesday will start at 7 pm.

Though most of the volunteers have graciously offered to stay on, Attila Plasch (treasurer) and I (president) have decided to step down after several years on the job.

Nominations began at the November meeting. New nominations will be accepted at the December meeting too. Currently standing for office are:

President: Steve Rodrigues – nominated and accepted
Vice-President: Chris Valley – nominated and accepted
Treasurer – Daniel Mizyrycki – nominated and accepted
Secretary - Gwenhaël Jacq – nominated and accepted
Safety – Tom White – nominated and accepted
Clubhouse – Brian Foster – nominated and accepted
Westlake – Rafael Lavin – nominated (not present to accept)

Thanks, and see you next week.

Daniel Pifko
