New Airspace Along the Funston Coast

Posted: Sat Dec 08, 2018 11:29 pm

The FAA has redefined the SFO TAC (airspace) and it changes how we fly the Funston coast. There are many new transition points where the ceiling changes, please see the attached photo. Note where the controlled airspace goes all the way down to the surface!
