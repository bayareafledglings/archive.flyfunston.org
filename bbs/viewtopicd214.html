2015 Funston Stickers!

Posted: Sat Nov 29, 2014 6:07 pm
by Steve Rodrigues
Fort Funston in 2015

Fort Funston has had one of the most consistent years of flying in its history. Almost every day was flyable this summer; and the good conditions continued right into the fall. Start planning now for next year by renewing your membership early and receiving your sticker by mail.

The details for “Stickers by mail” are here: http://flyfunston.org/sticker.php.html

Please note that the Fellow Feathers needs to keep better track of membership applications and hang gliding permits. As such, we will be limiting sticker distribution to a smaller number of club officers. That means that there will be many days when stickers will NOT be available on-site. If you really want to fly the Fort, it is highly recommended that you get your helmet sticker well before you intend to come out.

Thanks for preparing in advance. See you in the air!

The Fellow Feathers of Fort Funston Hang Gliding Club
