How to get a space in the clubhouse

by Steve Rodrigues » Sun Jun 17, 2007 8:29 am

Many pilots have been asking how they can get a space in the clubhouse to store their glider. Here's how;

First, join the club! Glider storage is on a space available basis only for active club members. You need to be flying the Fort on a regular basis, and it will be a benefit to everyone if you attend some club meetings, usually held in the clubhouse the second Tuesday of each month. Remember the free pizza and excellent camaraderie, not to mention having a say in what happens at our home site. Check this board for updates on meeting times.

The membership application is not yet available online but I can send one to you. Please email the president through our club officers contact page found at http://flyfunston.org/officers.php.html

Fill out the application and return it with payment and your request for a space in the clubhouse. You will then either be assigned a space, or be put on the waiting list, first come first served.

The waiting list will soon be kept on this FF discussion board and updated regularly.

If you have any questions, please contact FF President (Steve Rodrigues) or Clubhouse Director (Rafael Lavin).

Cheers!
