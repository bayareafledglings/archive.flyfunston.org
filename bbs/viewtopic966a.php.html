Unified Bay Area flying classifieds

Posted: Mon Apr 16, 2007 10:55 am
by Daniel Pifko
The recent discussion about FlightPlans and the WOR board made me think about what exactly is cross-posted across all local web sites on a regular basis.

Answer: For sale/wanted

I think most of us would be willing to make a long-ish trip from the south/east/north bay to buy a glider, even if we weren't interested in flying the other clubs' sites on a regular basis.

So what about having a single web site where people could post Greater Bay Area sale/wanted ads? Then each of the local clubs' for sale/wanted boards would go away and each club would link to the GBA web site.

Pilots who want to sell their gear would only have to post once to hit all the local and semi-local buyers.

The clubs I regularly surf for ads are Sonoma Wings, Funston, WOR and McClure/MLSR, so those are what I have in mind as participants.

I could set up a separate forum for this or we could put it on any one of the clubs' domains.

Thoughts?
