Steve Daleo

Posted: Wed Nov 01, 2006 4:06 pm
by Daniel Pifko
It is with sincere sorrow and regret that I post this.

I was recently informed that our friend and WOR President, Steve Daleo, suffered a fatal accident in an ultralight this past Saturday afternoon. Personally, I am stunned. Steve was a highly skilled aviator of multiple disciplines. I'm aware that an investigation of the cause is underway, and more information will be communicated as it is discovered.

Steve will certainly be missed by us all. At this point, I offer my deepest condolences to his Debra, his family, and his close friends.

Wayne M.

--------------

There will be a Memorial Service for Steve at the Resurrection Lutheran Church of Dublin this Thursday at 7pm.
