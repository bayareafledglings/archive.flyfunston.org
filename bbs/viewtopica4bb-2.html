Abandoned gliders to be sold

Posted: Mon Oct 19, 2009 11:20 am
by Steve Rodrigues
The clubhouse floor is NOT a storage space!

First: The floor space under the lowest glider rack is for the temporary placement of an outside space glider while the owner of the inside space gets his glider out. When there is a squatter on the floor, nobody in that entire glider rack has a place to set another glider.

Second: Gliders on the floor get wet and mildewed, transfer mold to other gliders and contribute to the overall humidity problem in the clubhouse.

The two gliders currently on the floor got wet during the last storm and are starting to mold. These gliders MUST be moved out ASAP.

Squatters have been somewhat neglected in the past but will no longer be tolerated. The club will impound and sell abandoned gliders after 30 days, per clubhouse rules.
