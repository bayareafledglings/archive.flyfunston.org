Fellow Feathers
10 April 2007

Present were:

Chris V.
David
Dwayne
Fred
Gwenhaël

Club house and associated items

- Rafi produces a paper list and discusses spot assignments.
- A proposal for clubhouse spot reform is passed around (see posting).
- Rafi will start to call people who are not current or are not flying.
- People who cannot retrieve their glider can have them delivered (locally) after they accept to release their spot.
- Currently, one needs to fly a minimum of 6 times a year in order to keep their spot.

Treasurer report and associated items

- Daniel M. is absent. No pizza is provided. That's one warning against Daniel

New business

- NY Dan suggests using smoke bombs to film rotors, landing patterns, etc. We need to obtain information/authorization from the park service.
- WOR fly-in is 22 April (see posting).
- Chris V. suggests adding a bench (~$700) by the setup area. A memorial plaque is also suggested and will probably require a new discussion.

Meeting adjourned.
