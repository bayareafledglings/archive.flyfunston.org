McClure Octoberfest 2013 is happening this weekend October 12th & 13th.

Go there, fly, and have fun...Aerosmith will be playing Saturday evening, or a dude with a tuba, so it's worth the trip...

I ran into Ranger Matt yesterday. He doesn't have a problem with us grabbing gliders from the clubhouse to take them out, which is what I was doing. You can park on the shoulder of the turn lane at the entrance, but do NOT block the gate nor park in front of the gate. A fire truck has to be able to get through easily. I didn't ask him whether he was sweeping people out of the park, but the park police I think warned our club not to "occupy" it. So you might want to be quick about it.
