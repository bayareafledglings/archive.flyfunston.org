Film Festival Workshop, The Ladies, June 28th

Posted: Tue Jun 19, 2012 5:28 pm
by davidroyer
Date/Time: Thursday, June 28th, 7:30 PM- 9:30 PM, 964 Natoma, San Francisco .

First time film-maker Anne Kroemer set a new standard in creativity at the 2011 NCFFFF with her music video 'The Ladies'. She wrote her own 'rap' and then worked with Brad Halverson, composer, to create the beats.

Anne will share thoughts on her process regarding project planning, music creation and editing, showing how someone with zero video or editing experience can create a film on a laptop in a few weeks.

As always, Eric Blum will moderate and kick off the evening.

Location: 964 Natoma Street, San Francisco

Cheers, NCFFFF Team.
