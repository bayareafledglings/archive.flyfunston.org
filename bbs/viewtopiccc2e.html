too bad the wind shut off as myself and Richard and Kurtis got set up around 3 pm.
the hang waiting was spectacular for the visibility below a cloud deck . We could clearly see the houses in Marin at Muir Beach , 10 miles away. there's a bluff on the south side of MUir Beach that looked to be about 3 miles away but was 9.5
at one point the sun was shining only on the GG Bridge like a movie spotlight. Urs got some Contour footage which should be eminently viewable.
