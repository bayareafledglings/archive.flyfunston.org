June 2005: Name Change Poll
This poll is to determine whether the name of the organization should be changed to the most popular alternate name "United States Hang Gliding and Paragliding Association", or whether it should remain unchanged. This poll will end on October 1, 2005. If more the 50% of the responding members favor the name change, the next step in the process will be an official vote of the membership to start in January of 2006. To participate in the poll click here.

You'll need a www.ushga.org password to vote. They're easy to get - just call USHGA or use the contact form to get in touch.
