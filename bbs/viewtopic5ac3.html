Helmet stickers by mail!

Posted: Mon Nov 03, 2008 5:54 pm
by Steve Rodrigues
Get a jump on 2009 by getting your helmet stickers by mail!

Simply fill out a waiver:

Link to Waiver http://flyfunston.org/documents/ffwaiver.pdf

Then, fill out a sticker application:

Link to sticker application http://flyfunston.org/documents/ffsticker.pdf

Include a copy of your USHPA card and a self-addressed stamped envelope and your good to go!

Mail waiver, sticker application, and USHPA information to:

Chris Valley
107 Madison Avenue
San Mateo, CA 94402

Thanks,

Chris
Fellow Feathers

P.S...If you have never flown Fort Funston, you must have a site introduction. Contact a club Site Mentor for your site introduction...We'll be happy to help! Link to Mentor page; http://flyfunston.org/mentors.php.html[/url]
