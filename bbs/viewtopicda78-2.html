Clubhouse spaces

by charlie nelson » Tue Jan 06, 2009 2:07 am

HG is about air time . The more the better. Funston is perhaps the best place on the West coast to build your hours and experience.
There are 9 pilots seeking a storage slot in the clubhouse.
These nine individuals love to fly Fabulous Funston . Most of them are enthusiastic new pilots.
The club is contacting existing storage users who may not have been in the air 20 hours last year and asking them to let the more active pilots have their spaces.
If you know that you won't be able to fly the minimum hours in 2009 , please contact me.
A new or returning pilot will be elated to be able to move in. You will get the satisfaction of helping the air sportsmen of today experience the wonder of the Fort.
Plus free pizza at the club meeting .
Later , when you return to active flying , you will be able to obtain a spot.
See you on top of the next Spring shear.
Charlie Nelson
clubhouse manager
