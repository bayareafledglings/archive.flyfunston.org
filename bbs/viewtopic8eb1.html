Nor-Cal Free Flight Film Festival!

Posted: Wed Nov 20, 2013 4:41 pm
by Steve Rodrigues
Pilots,

We are excited to announce the Nor Cal Free Flight Film Festival V, Jan 18th, 2014, 6:00 PM in a classic San Francisco location, the Victoria Theater!

Due to limited seating, please purchase your tickets today at http://www.norcalfreeflight.com, and bring a copy (paper or digital) of your email receipt for entry. Seating for ticket holders is guaranteed.

Also, Event T-shirts and Hoodies are available for purchase on the website, please bring your receipt for pick-up at the festival.

Come and celebrate this year’s adventures, and vote for your favorite film in the Audience Award Category!

If you have something to share, submit a film by the deadline of Dec 31st, and win prizes from GoPro, Patagonia, USHPA, and other sponsors.

We need your help for this non-profit, pilot-driven event, if you can assist please email: filmfestival@norcalfreeflight.com

Please enjoy last year’s NCFF IV Winner from the Sierra Safari team, a story about a cross-country paraglide expedition in the Sierras.

Cheers,

NCFF.
