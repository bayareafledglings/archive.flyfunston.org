Chahhhhlie Nelson is the right person to speak to, since he's the keeper of the list and has the most up to date info with zero hearsay. Contact info at memberlist.php.html?mode=viewprofile&u=13 .

For the immediate term, you may need to find someone with a spare spot or a garage.

For the clubhouse, it usually clears out in one big wave each year when people don't re-up rather than bit by bit, so get on that wait list if you aren't already.

As for the 20 hour thing - that's not meant for pilots new to Funston or moving into the area, so don't sweat it too much.
