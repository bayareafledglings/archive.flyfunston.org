Get a jump on the next year by getting your helmet stickers by mail!

Simply fill out a an application package. Get it here: http://flyfunston.org/sticker.php.html

Include a copy of your USHPA card and a self-addressed stamped envelope and you're good to go!

Mail waiver, sticker application, donation/dues and USHPA information to:

Chris Valley
107 Madison Avenue
San Mateo, CA 94402
