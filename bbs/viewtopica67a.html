Chip moving project update

Posted: Mon Nov 04, 2013 3:45 pm
by LadyHawk
We've had 3 very successful chip moving parties to date and tomorrow (11/4) Steve R and I are going to meet at the Fort at 11am to do some remaining raking that's needed. If one or two others wish to join us, that would be great, but if not, Steve and I can probably handle it.

The GGNRA will then visit the site, probably in the next week or two, to inspect the areas we've worked on and they'll let us know if the changes made are sufficient for their needs. We will get back to you with an update once we have one from them. Hopefully after tomorrow's raking we will be done with the project and won't need the scheduled 11/16/13 chip moving party.

Thanks again to all the pilots who pitched in to help with this project! We have a lot of fantastic people in this club.
