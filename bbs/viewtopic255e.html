LOCATION FOR DECEMBER'S MONTHLY CLUB MEETING

Posted: Tue Nov 19, 2013 10:05 pm
by LadyHawk
At our November monthly club meeting the members present voted on whether it would be prudent for us to hold our next few monthly meetings offsite. The overwhelming majority voted to do so, so our December monthly club meeting will be held at the Moose Lodge in Pacifica, located at 776 Bradford Street, Pacifica, CA 94044. This is the same location the November meeting was held at.

Directions: If coming from FWY-280, take Hwy 1 south. Take the Sharp Park Exit (same exit as for the Sharp Park Golf Course). At the foot of the off-ramp, turn left on Bradford and go 0.3 miles. The Moose Lodge will be on the left.

The 12/10/13 meeting will start at 7:30 pm as usual.

We've reserved this location for January, February & March's meetings as well.

We will pass a hat for donations at each meeting to pay the $50 to use their facility.
