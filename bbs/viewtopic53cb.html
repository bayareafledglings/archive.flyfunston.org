Posted: Sat Sep 05, 2009 11:06 am
by fakeDecoy
For your first flight watch the weather graph on the webcam page for W to SW wind, with the red line solidly above 10mph. There are several other good ways to help forecast over the next few days, but to start with it's easiest just to go out there when it's already good.

You get the lemming effect with the webcam. It's not uncommon to see 15 gliders set up in non soarable conditions. But sometimes you can catch a shot of a glider soaring.
