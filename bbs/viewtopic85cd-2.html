Attention.. Pilots with gliders in the clubhouse..

Please add identification to your glider the next time you are in the clubhouse. It's required that you have some form of ID on your glider so it can be identified.. Lately there have been gliders in the wrong spot and it makes sorting this out difficult when I have no idea who owns the wing.

So please check your glider next time and add something if needed..

Thanks..
