Hat Creek Rim News

Posted: Thu Oct 14, 2010 6:12 pm
by flyingbrian
Hat Creek Rim News:

The Forest Service is requiring site insurance and an operating plan at Hat Creek Rim. Pilots flying Hat Creek Rim will now need to be current USHPA members, hold a minimum H3/P3 rating, and sign a waiver. A H2/P2 mentoring program is available.

The waiver and sticker information can be found at www.hatcreek.info. Stickers will be valid for the calendar year in which they are issued and a $10.00 donation is requested to cover site operation and maintenance costs. The 2011 sticker is available now.

If you have any questions feel free to contact flyingbrian17@hotmail.com. More details can also be found at www.hatcreek.info
