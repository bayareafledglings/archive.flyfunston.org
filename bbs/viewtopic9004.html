Change of date for St Helena fly-in

Posted: Fri Apr 29, 2016 1:25 pm
by Mike K
Due to the high wind forecast of 20-30 mph in the LZ, we have decided to postpone the St Helena fly-in originally scheduled for April 30, May 1 until May 7, 8.
We all felt not only would the flying be non existent, but it would also be too uncomfortable to eat and party.

This year Sonoma Wings will supply drinks, chips and BBQ meats for everyone, and we are asking for other pot luck dishes to round out the menu. The fly-in is open to all, with a few restrictions on flying. Visit sonomawings.com for more detail and to sign up.

The folks below signed up on the SW bulletin board to fly/eat, but we have no contact information for them. If you know any of these folks please pass along the change of date to them.
