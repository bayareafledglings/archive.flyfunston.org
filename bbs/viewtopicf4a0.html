GGNRA waiver GONE!

by Steve Rodrigues » Fri Feb 25, 2011 5:20 pm

To all Funston pilots,

After untold time and effort and the generous help of USHPA attorney Tim Herrs' pro-bono contribution, I have gotten the GGNRA to drop their requirement for their Fort Funston hang gliding waiver!

Determination from the NPS, February 25, 2011:
"Effective immediately, Fellow Feathers is instructed to discontinue use of a GGNRA waiver."

This determination will reduce the clubs annual printing expenses, archiving requirements, and every Funston pilots waiver headache!

Note: All Funston pilots must be current members of the USHPA.

Regards,
Steve Rodrigues
Fellow Feathers liaison to the GGNRA
USHPA # 30605
H-5, Mentor and Observer
