Olympic Club Event Parking

Posted: Wed Aug 22, 2007 5:09 pm
by Dan Brown
The Olympic Club has a permit to use the lot 8.18-26. The permit requires that parking attendants "ensure that all parking on north and west sides of Fort Funston lot remain open and accessible for regular park visitors." Parking will not block access to the "hang gliding barn".

It seems unusual that a private golf club has been allowed to reserve part of a public parking lot for spectators attending its golf tournament while apparently prohibiting spectators parking on its property including its parking lot. Nevertheless the GGNRA has been an excellent landlord and a good friend of hang gliding and we should be greatful for its stewardship.

Dan Brown
