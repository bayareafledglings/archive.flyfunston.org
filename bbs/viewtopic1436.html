Everyone's seen and read my post about breaking an upright last Saturday. Here's the rest of the story...

I love these Moyes gliders!! They are strong and simple designs that just don't have any bad manners. They do what they are told -certainly there are times I tell it the wrong thing! But it is a pure joy to fly!!!

Now for Moyes America and Ken Brown...

I broke the upright last Saturday. Went home and checked the glider for other unseen damage. Logged onto MoyesAmerica and ordered the part online that night. It arrived Wednesday morning at my doorstep. And Thursday was a great day at Funston!!!

Thanks Ken!
