A local pilot at Indian Valley requested I post this...

The Indian Valley bailout LZ - aka "Tweetins" - is closed - done - no more - finished.

The new ownership of that land is not as accepting as the prior owner to our sport - so there you have it.

If you fly Indian Valley - get a site intro from a pilot familiar with the area - the Sonoma Wingers frequent that area - check their bulletin board for flying trips, etc.
