DALY CITY: COAST GUARD RESCUES STRANDED HANG GLIDER
DALY CITY (BCN)

The U.S. Coast Guard saved a stranded hang glider Sunday after he
crashed on a cliff in Daly City near Mussel Rock, according to the
U.S. Coast Guard.
The Coast Guard responded with a helicopter around 7 p.m. after it was
determined that the fire department could not reach the man.
A rescuer was lowered to the cliff and hoisted the man so he could be
transferred to the beach, where paramedics were waiting, the Coast
Guard reported.
The man was not injured, according to the Coast Guard.
