The temperature/humidity sensor on our weather station is hosed and it's confusing the rest of the system. We're getting a new sensor from Davis Instruments (under warranty), which should arrive and be installed in the next few days. In the meanwhile the wind readings on the web are completely offline. The (voice) windtalker is working and not affected by this issue.
