MIKE PELTON
November 14, 1954 – September 4, 2004



A memorial celebration and remembrance of Mike’s life will be held in the

Giant Cluster picnic area at

Point Pinole Regional Shoreline in Richmond, CA on

Sunday, September 26, 2004,

from 1:00 – 5:00 p.m.(please note the time change)



We hope you can join us and share your memories…

As Mike would have preferred, the dress is casual, and it is a potluck, with volleyball and horseshoes nearby. There will undoubtly be plenty for all. However, if you would like to coordinate your contribution to the potluck, please contact “Mo” at (925) 258-0266, e-mail: mocollins99@yahoo.com

Mike will be honored by the planting of a redwood grove on his property in Kettenpom. Donations may be made to the “Mike Pelton Memorial Redwood Grove Fund” and sent c/o Dave and Chris Lubeck, 103 Edelen Avenue #1, Los Gatos, CA 95030.

A second celebration and remembrance is planned for Noon, October 3, 2004, at the Kettenpom Park/Store in the Trinity County area of Northern California.

For a MAP showing directions to Point Pinole please go to: http://www.ebparks.org, select Maps, then scroll down to Pt. Pinole.

or here are directions:

From I-80 in Richmond: Take the Richmond Parkway exit and go west towards the Bay. Cross San Pablo Avenue and watch for the Pt. Pinole/Giant Highway exit. Turn right (north) at Giant Highway. The park entrance will be ahead on the left, past the railroad tracks.

From I-580 east of the Richmond/San Rafael Bridge: Exit at Richmond Parkway/Castro Street and go north for about 5 miles on Richmond Parkway. Exit at Giant Highway and turn right. The park entrance will be ahead on the left, past the railroad tracks.[/b]
