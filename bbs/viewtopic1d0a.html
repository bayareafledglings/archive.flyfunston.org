Temporary Flight Restriction - July 22 & 23

Posted: Mon Jul 21, 2014 1:15 am
by Wingman
The FAA has issued the text for an upcoming VIP visit to the Bay Area.

TFR begins Tuesday July 22 at 2045 PDT and ends Wednesday July 23 at 1345 PDT. The TFR is described as a 30 nautical mile radius around the lat long 37.802,-122.4689; The vertical limits are the surface up to (but not including) 18,000 feet. Within this volume, during the the specified period, hang gliding and "parachute activities" are prohibited.

Again, the times are:

Start: Tuesday 8:45pm
End: Wednesday 1:45pm
