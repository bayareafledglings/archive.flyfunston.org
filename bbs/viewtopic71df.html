Communicating with the GGNRA

Posted: Tue Sep 24, 2013 8:53 am
by LadyHawk
I received a call from the GGNRA asking that I relay their request below to our Club members:

1) If there are any issues at Fort Funston where someone’s safety is in question, like a potential physical altercation, or a car is vandalized, etc, that their Law Enforcement dispatch be called. The yellow phone by the water fountain is a hotline to their Law Enforcement dispatch.

2) For any issues other than safety issues, like park maintenance, special use permit, Club house concerns, etc, that the member bring their concern to the Club’s Executive Committee or Club’s GGNRA Liaison instead of directly to the GGNRA.

Their request is that member’s concerns are communicated to them from only one to two points of contact from our Club. That would be the Club’s GGNRA Liaison or President.

Thanks for not shooting the messenger,
Tracey
