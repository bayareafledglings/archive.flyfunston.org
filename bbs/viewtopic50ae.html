Glider Purgatory

Posted: Sun Apr 30, 2006 7:59 am
by Steve Rodrigues
We currently have 15 gliders in the clubhouse impound rack. (Glider Purgatory). Some of them appear to be abandoned, but some of the gliders belong to pilots who were not flying much and gave up their spots to more active pilots. Because of their consideration, we are storing their gliders temporarily. These gliders are not abandoned!!! Please do not touch any gliders in Purgatory, as they are personal property.

We will soon post a list of "abandoned" gliders in a last attempt to find the owners. Unclaimed gliders will go up for auction in conjunction with the October 10 club meeting and BBQ.

Steve Rodrigues, V.P.
