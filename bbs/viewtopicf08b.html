NCFF Film Festival 2012 Update

Posted: Sun Dec 09, 2012 9:25 pm
by davidroyer
Fellow Pilots,

We are looking forward to seeing you at the 2012 Nor Cal Free Flight Film Festival, taking place next Sat, Dec 15th, with doors opening at 6:00 PM. There is limited indoor seating, theater doors will be closed either when venue capacity is reached, or at 7 PM sharp, when the show begins.

Weather permitting, we will be setting up an outdoor film screen and bar. For guests expecting to arrive after 7 PM, please bring a camping chair, and dress warm.

For indoor seating, $15 donation will be accepted at the door. Donations, beer, wine, NCFF 2012 Hoodies and T-shirts are cash-only.

Following the Awards Ceremony, we plan to turn up the music, raise a glass, and celebrate with the 2012 NCFF Filmmakers, the venue is available until 2 AM.

This event is made possible by 100% volunteer effort. If you would like to participate, please email filmfestival@norcalfreeflight.com.

Cheers, NCFF Team.
