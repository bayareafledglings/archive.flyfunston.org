Just a reminder: If you choose to leave your wallet in your car, be sure to carry your personal ID, USHPA card, Tandem Exemption, plus any insurance cards you might need in case of an accident.
(The best idea might be to just not carry a lot of cash in your wallet.)

If you do leave valuables in your car, be sure they are not visible!
