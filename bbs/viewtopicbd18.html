Tom LOw posted this on FB today ...I hope he doesn't mind my reposting it..and i quote Tom.

Awesome day at Funston. Some excitement early on, as I was the first to launch in light Northy breeze. Just passing the drainpipe, I see a guy on the beach waiving frantically, yelling at me to get out of the air. Recalling the SFPD cru...iser in the parking lot, and noting the news helicopter flying nearby, I opted to heed his advice and top landed ASAP. Several minutes later, a huge explosion rocked the cliff, and a cloud of smoke drifts up the face of the cliff. I suspect recent storms have unearthed WWII munitions, and the bomb disposal guys were working. After the chopper left, we all returned to the air for the rest of the afternoon.
