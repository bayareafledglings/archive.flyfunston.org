PG Fly By

Posted: Fri Aug 22, 2008 8:33 pm
by Steve Rodrigues
A PG pilot I know just called to ask permission to do a PG group fly-by in honor of Dan Murphy.

I am quite aware of the sensitivity of this request but also respect their gesture.

I told him that our special use permit prohibited PG flying at Fort Funston but in the name of camaraderie and good will, it would be OK for them to come to the north end of the training bowl on this one time special occasion.

They will plan on coming from the Dumps as a group, making a few passes, then heading back south.

Having just experienced a reminder of my own mortality I can testify that this life has no time for prejudice.
Please hold this gesture as a salute to our fallen comrade.

PS; I'll ask around at the BBQ for feedback about this and call it off if too much negative response.
