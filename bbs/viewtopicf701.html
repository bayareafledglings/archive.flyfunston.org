weekends are good. winter sucks.

Posted: Mon Nov 26, 2007 10:18 am
by Daniel Pifko
Unfortunately, winter is not the best season for this. We need daylight and west-ish winds of 14-25ish mph to be able to fly. At this time of year weekend afternoons will be your best bet. There isn't usually enough daylight for people to come out after work as they do during the summer. On http://flyfunston.org/newwebcam/ you should look for winds of that speed and direction and, of course, for gliders in the webcam. For forecasts, look at http://www.wrh.noaa.gov/forecast/MapCli ... 362&zone=1 for predictions of southwest, west or northwest and/or afternoon seabreeze.
