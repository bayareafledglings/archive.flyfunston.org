Message from FF President - elections 2013

by tom rust » Fri Dec 07, 2012 5:30 pm

Once again Fellow Feathers elections are coming at the next meeting on Dec 11. Please come to this important meeting to have your voice heard and your vote counted.

I am running again for President. I appreciate the support I have had in the past and it is my desire once again to help as a voice for those who regularly fly Funston and want someone to have their voice heard. We've had a lot of fun at the Fort over the last 2 years I've been president.
Some of the work I've done for the club includes:
- The Westlakecam
- Repairs to the wind speed indicator, anemometer, temperature & humidity sensor, and the windsock.
- The 2012 Air Races
- Numerous BBQs - buying the food, set up & teardown
- Maintenance on the drinking fountain
- Keeping the observation pit alive
- Dealing with more bs than I ever cared to.
- Helping keep Funston a fun place to fly

I'm looking forward to a fun 2013! With the increased number of storms its likely we'll have more good post frontal days in the months ahead. I'm looking forward to some killer sheers. Come join us at the Fort for another year of awesome air.
tom rust
