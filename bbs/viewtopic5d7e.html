Debbie the wind talker is up and running!

by Steve Rodrigues » Tue Feb 26, 2008 11:22 pm

This afternoon/evening I took down the old anemometer and wires, got the new anemometer mounted on the mast and aimed west, ran the cable down the mast inside the protective cover, fished it under the eaves into the clubhouse, found the wires too short to reach the custom junction box I made to adapt the new phone jack to the old dual stereo plugs, re-routed the cable from the windtalker through the rafters across the entire clubhouse so it would reach the junction box, plugged in the connections, taped and zip tied everything in place and got Debbie up and running!
Yeah, no big deal.
