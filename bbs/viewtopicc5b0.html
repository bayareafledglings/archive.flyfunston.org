Regional PG/HG Pilot Summit.

by ben » Fri Oct 21, 2005 2:24 pm

This will be a big event. Don't miss it.


What: An region-wide summit for HG/PG pilots. Participating clubs include WOR, Fellow Feathers, BAPA, California Condors. Invitees include Sonoma Wings, MotherLode Sky Riders, and any other NorCal club or pilot. Attend the potluck, educational and entertainment events, get new site and event info, renew your membership and get stickers, or just hang out with your fellow pilots. Families and non-pilots are welcome.

When: Dec. 2 6:30-11:30

Where: Palo Alto Community Center. 3800 Middlefield Road Palo Alto, CA (between Meadow Drive and Charleston Road). The community center is adjacent to Mitchell park and its fields, picnic area, playground, etc. ( see http://www.cityofpaloalto.org/community ... rkmap1.pdf)
