Wills Wing demos this weekend at funston

by patdenevan » Thu May 12, 2005 4:02 pm

Rob Kells,president of Wills Wing, will be visiting Funston this weekend May 14th and 15th. He is bringing one each of their latest production gliders, including the Talon 2 144 and 154. Besides the Sport 2 155 he will bring the smaller size Sport 2 135. Both sizes of the U2 will be there also. Rob will even have a couple sizes of Attack Falcon.
If we get south winds Wills wing demo days will move to Ed Levin Park.
