October 2005 Clinics!

Posted: Sat Sep 24, 2005 10:38 am
by MissionSoaringCent
Mission Soaring Center will be hosting 3 upcoming clinics that may be of interest to you! If you would like to sign up for one of these clinics, please call and reserve your spot ASAP!

October 15-17:
Instructor Training Clinic (call for reservations)
A three day program on October 15th, 16th and 17th availible to anyone who is a Hang 3 or higher and is interested in becoming a USHGA Instructor.

October 20:
Parachute Clinic (call for reservations)
Observe chute packing technique. Practice throwing your chute in a simulated emergency. Lecture and discussion format covering chute technology and safe practices. $50 or free with parachute purchase.

October 29-30:
Tandem Clinic (call for reservations)
USHGA tandem clinic for H3s and up that will cover tandem 1, 2 and tandem instructor ratings. Classroom portion of the class will be 9am at MSC on Saturday morning.
