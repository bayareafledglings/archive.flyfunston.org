there I sat.....

by diev » Tue Jan 04, 2005 9:22 pm

By the time I was at launch it was southwest at around 5-7mph, I set up my moto and played a bit, wished it would go faster (just like the real thing I MORE SPEED).....so after a bit it seemed to pic up another couple miles an hour, the wind was from an ok direction still so what the heck. During my setup I noticed it getting a bit stronger, maybe 8 or 9mph now, thinking it would keep getting stronger I smiled and felt all happy, BUT by the time I was ready it backed off and just lingered around 6 or 7 again. I hooked in and went to launch, told the guys to keep flying (RCs), I just wanted to feel the air. So there I sat, all ready but not willing to go to the beach for the first time so I packed it up. I did get some awesome pictures, I'll have to add one to my funston page on my site....
