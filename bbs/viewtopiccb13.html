Dog Management Plan

Posted: Sat Dec 24, 2011 5:12 am
by Steve Rodrigues
In case anyone is wondering it they should be holding their breath or not, the GGNRA dog management plan is here: http://www.nps.gov/goga/parkmgmt/dog-management.htm


Dog management timeline:
January 2012: Publish project newsletter outlining possible changes to the alternatives based on public comment and next steps in the process.
Late Summer/Early Fall 2012: Publish Notice of Proposed Rulemaking for public comment
Late Fall 2012: Incorporate public comment on Proposed Rulemaking into the Final Environmental Impact Statement (FEIS)for dog management.
First half of 2013: Finalize FEIS
Late Summer 2013: Publish Notice of Availability of FEIS
End of 2013: Final rule published. Implementation begins 30 days after rule published.
All times are UTC - 8 hours [ DST ]
