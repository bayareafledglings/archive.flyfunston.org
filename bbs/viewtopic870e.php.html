A couple more thoughts:

- The resolution on these cameras isn't fantastic - maybe .38 megapixels, if I'm reading it right. We need better resolution than that for our purpose. The cam in the clubhouse is 7MP. Do they have a higher res version?

- I have to say, the IQEye cam we have in the Ropes building (the view of the setup area only) has been totally headache free. It's not as good a resolution as the one in the clubhouse, but it's pretty reasonable (1024x768, maybe?) an it being in a harder-to-reach spot means uptime is crucial. We don't want to have to go out there once a month or so just to reset the thing. In the two years it's been in, it hasn't gone down once. BTW, it was graciously donated, completely gratis, by a pilot down in SoCal. What a guy!
