Hat Creek "Best of Lift" fly in Sept 27th

Posted: Thu Sep 11, 2014 7:47 am
by strawfly
The postponed Best of Lift fly in has been rescheduled for Sept 27th 2014.

Huge fires shut down Hat Creek for about a month. Although tragically the local store/cafe and some other residences burnt down the flying site (launch and LZ's)
are unscathed.

This fly in, in part to remember well known pilot Page Perrin, who lived at Hat Creek for over 20 years and was the face and personality that most pilots would see at Hat Creek, and to raise funds for Hat Creeks future.

New T shirts will be available as well as a new batch of last years green ones that Page had made.

A pot luck is planned and we are working on having some BBQ of some kind.

Roger Jackson
