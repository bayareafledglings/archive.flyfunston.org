Some general info

Well, we are just a few days away from the 2015 Coyote howl.

Some very important safety information for our low air time pilots. The site is active and very soar-able after 10am daily, with that the LZ becomes active early as well. You will be asked to launch early … however there will be rides to launch all day long.

I suggest taking a morning flight, breakdown, pack up and get your wing back on a truck for the afternoon flight. Again conditions will be evaluated for our low time pilots.

$8 MID Day use fee Mandatory per vehicle
$30 to enter into the competition and a chance to take home one or more of 12 Trophies. ( You do not have to compete, just pay the day use and Fly )

Speed Glide
Iron Pilot
Spot landing
Cross Country
Pylon Course
Horseshoe Dash

Food

Hamburgers, Turkey burgers, chicken will be provided. The only thing we ask people to bring is their own beverages and a side of their choosing.

$10 MLSR day use for FLYING ONLY !

$20 MLSR T- shirts go fast every year make sure you get one early !

Schedule
Saturday May 16

0700 – 0745 registration
0755 Pilot meeting in the LZ
0800 rides to launch from LZ for speed glide
0900 – 0930 speed glide window

All Low time / first flight at McClure Need to launch Before 10 am. The club will allow prime time flying for H2’s If you instructor or observer rated sponsor vouches via email txt or in person if the conditions allow.

Prime flying at McClure normally starts in the afternoon around 2pm

Evening glass off can start as early as 4pm … again conditions must be evaluated.
