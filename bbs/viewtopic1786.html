Tandem clinics!

by Steve Rodrigues » Mon Apr 05, 2010 6:09 am

T-1 TANDEM HG CLINIC May 14-15, 2010

USHPA Tandem Administrator Steve Rodrigues will be offering a T-1 clinic for qualified hang glider pilots, to be held in the San Francisco Bay Area on Friday evening May 14, and all day Saturday May 15. Cost is $150.00 per person, includes study materials and use of WW Tandem Falcon. BYOH&H (bring your own harness and helmet). More info at: www.skypuppy.us email: steve@skypuppy.us



T-2/T-3 TANDEM INSTRUCTOR HG CLINIC September 17-19, 2010

USHPA Tandem Administrator Steve Rodrigues will be offering a T-2 and T-3 (Tandem Instructor) clinic for qualified T-1 hang glider pilots, to be held in the San Francisco Bay Area, Friday evening Sept. 17, and all day Saturday and Sunday Sept 18+19. Cost is $350.00 per person**, includes all study materials. BYOH,H&TG(bring your own harness, helmet and tandem glider). **$50.00 discount for previous students. More info at: www.skypuppy.us email: steve@skypuppy.us
