Save Point of the Mountain

Posted: Sat Mar 16, 2013 11:53 am
by Steve Rodrigues
To all pilots,

One of the biggest concerns pilots talk about is site preservation. Now you have a chance to do something about it! We need your help to save Point of the Mountain in Utah.

Anyone who has flown the North Side knows what an amazing flying site it is. The main reason the site is so great is our ability to “Bench Up” and fly the main ridge behind launch. The gravel company who owns the land is planning to quarry out the mountain behind launch! This would create a huge gap in the ridge and would be catastrophic to our flying there.

Please follow the link below where you can see a graphic example of what they are doing and what you can do to help stop them. Please, if nothing else, sign the on-line petition.

http://www.change.org/petitions/salt-la ... p-mountain

Thank you!
Steve Rodrigues
