Important Club Meeting This Tuesday!

by LadyHawk » Fri Oct 04, 2013 5:46 pm

This coming Tuesday's club meeting (10/8) is vitally important. Our special use permit and site insurance are seriously at risk and we will be discussing those concerns.

USHPA's President, Rich Hass, will be attending our meeting to address concerns that have put our site insurance at risk. Mitch Shipley, USHPA's appointed Investigative Officer for the upcoming hearings will attend our meeting as well.

We will also vote in a new VP. Patrick has served our club well this year, but due to extensive travel requirements that have been implemented in his job, he no longer has the time necessary to serve as VP and has stepped down. Thank you for having been there for our club, Patrick! You'll be missed.

The location of Tuesday's meeting is to be determined. We are in the process of arranging an alternative place to meet should the government shutdown still be in effect at that time. We'll announce the location once we've firmed it up. The meeting, as usual, will be from 7:30pm to 9pm.

Due to the Government shut down, the October 8 club meeting will be held at the Sharp Park Golf Course in Pacifica, on the ocean side of Hwy 1 at Sharp Park Rd., from 7:30 to 9:30 PM. Pizza will be served!

This is a critical meeting and your vote is very important. Please come and help save Fort Funston!

Note: GPS navigation does not work well for the street address, it will put you in the ocean.

Directions from Fort Funston, 12 minutes:
Head South on Skyline Blvd., Take the Hwy 1 exit South towards Pacifica, go 3.4 miles and take the Sharp Park Exit. At the end of the off ramp turn Right on Bradford, go 300’ and the golf course driveway entrance will be on your left.

From San Francisco/north bay:
Take 280 South, exit Hwy 1 south towards Pacifica, go 4.5 miles and take the Sharp Park Exit. At the end of the off ramp turn Right onto Bradford, go 300’ and the golf course driveway entrance will be on your left.

From the south bay:
Take 280 North, exit Hwy 1 south towards Pacifica, go 4.5 miles and take the Sharp Park Exit. At the end of the off ramp turn Right onto Bradford, go 300’ and the golf course driveway entrance will be on your left.

From Half Moon Bay:
Take Hwy 1 North, Take the Sharp Park Exit. At the end of the off ramp turn left on Lundy, at the stop sign turn left on Sharp Park Rd. and cross over Hwy 1. Turn Left on Bradford, go 700‘ and the driveway entrance will be on your right.

Re: Important Club Meeting This Tuesday!

by Steve Rodrigues » Wed Oct 09, 2013 4:58 pm

I want to thank the many folks who made the trek to Pacifica last night. The meeting was a great success and the overwhelming majority sent out a clear message.

The club put some stronger rules in place and the FF EC is committed to a zero-tolerance policy. I believe that the clubs actions will put to rest USHPA's insurance concerns and the concerns of the NPS/GGNRA.

In addition, two long-time Funston pilots will be the focus of a USHPA disciplinary hearing at the BOD meeting this week.

Things are changing at the Fort and I feel it is safe to say that the quality of life at Funston will be very much better next year!

Again, Thank you all for helping save the site!
