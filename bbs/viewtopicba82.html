FMI: The link to the FAA listing of the Temporary Flight Restriction (TFR) is: http://tfr.faa.gov/save_pages/detail_3_2907.html

The effectivity for the TFR is 11/25/2013 10:15 PST to 15:59 PST.

On the Hang Gliding/Paragliding side of things we are effectively shut-down for the day at:

Fort Funston (including The Dumps)
Ed Levin
Mission Ridge
Mt Tam
Mt Diablo
Mission Ridge

Waddell Creek looks to be just outside the 32NM zone of confusion (TFR).
