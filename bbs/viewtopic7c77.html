2006 Funston Stickers

Posted: Mon Dec 19, 2005 11:03 pm
by Steve Rodrigues
The 2006 Funston stickers are out, and we distributed packets of them to most of the board members at the December meeting.

Pilots who have Funston stickers from 2005 can now get their 2006 stickers by mail! I'm keeping it to renewing pilots only to make sure new pilots get their stickers from a real live person who can give them a site intro.

Here is how to get your sticker:

Print out a sticker application and waiver. They can be found at

http://flyfunston.org/documents/ffsticker.pdf

and

http://flyfunston.org/documents/ffwaiver.pdf

Include the following:

A copy of your current USHGA card,
A SASE (self addressed stamped envelope),
No fee is charged but contributions to cover the cost of the program are greatly appreciated.


Send it all to me and I'll get your sticker on the way ASAP!
