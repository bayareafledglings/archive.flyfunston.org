Hi Jon,

I'm from San Diego, but I have really enjoyed the flying (and the club) at Funston. I hope you will too.

Marina, Big Sur, and Lakeview are also excellent. You may especially like Crestline because they have excellent on-site camping arrangements.

You might also add Sylmar and possibly Torrey Pines to the list. It turns out that the Torrey Hawks Hang Gliding Club has a monthly club meeting / fly-in on the Second Sunday of every month with free food and soda. In June, that will be Sunday the 9th. Torrey is an H4 site, but they sometimes allow H3 pilots with sufficient air time to fly. You might also want to check the wind forecast if you're driving out of your way, but it is one of the west coast's hang gliding landmarks.

The Torrey Hawks forum is at http://ushawks.org/forum/viewforum.php.html?f=4, and you're welcome to post there as well.

I have to say that I'm jealous of your trip. It sounds epic, and I'm sure it will generate a lifetime of memories. Best wishes, and fly safely.

Bob Kuczewski
