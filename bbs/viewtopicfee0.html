Gathering in Remembrance of Anet James, Sunday Sept 28

Posted: Tue Sep 16, 2014 3:27 am
by Henry Bittner
Friends and fellow pilots: We are having a Gathering in Remembrance of Anet James on Sunday Sept 28th 1- 5pm, at the Stern Grove Trocadero Clubhouse, Pass the word.
All are welcome to share their memories though stories, song, photos and the fellowship of this gathering to honor her beautiful life. If you get a chance, please RSVP to roxchow at yahoo dot com so we can plan for refreshments. This is a causal gathering as Anet would have it be. Come as you are.

The Trocadero Clubhouse is located in Stern Grove:
Enter Stern Grove from the corner of 19th ave and Sloat Blvd,
or from pathway down from Wawona St enter path through woods between 21st Ave and 22nd Ave,
There is also a parking lot at the bottom of Vale Ave, off of Sloat Blvd, but it is often full (see map).
Closest street address is 2750 19th Ave
San Francisco, CA 94110
corner of 19th Ave & Sloat Blvd
