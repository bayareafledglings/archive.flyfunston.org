Rob Johnson and the FF Clubhouse

Posted: Tue Feb 21, 2012 3:04 pm
by Steve Rodrigues
I’d like to bring everyone’s attention to the recent improvements to our clubhouse.

If you recall, last year Rob Johnson almost singlehandedly refurbished the building; framing in windows, sealing the mural, painting the entire exterior, applying anti-mould enzymes to the interior, and too much more to detail.

Last week he attacked it again! Rob donated and hung two beautiful and very useful storage cabinets for us. He wrangled his own helper to re-arrange the lockers, hang the cabinets and clean up the clubhouse. The place really looks great!

Rob doesn’t even keep a glider in the clubhouse, so this is clearly an act of hang glider altruism. Please give him a big “Thank you” next time you see him!
