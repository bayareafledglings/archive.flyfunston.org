new rule proposed

by Steve Rodrigues » Thu Sep 03, 2009 11:17 am

Work on our new Special Use Permit was suspended after the incident on July 11. What hurt us more than violating the 25' rule was the fact that pilots in the LZ stonewalled the Park Police and refused to identify the pilot in question. This is what caused the chief of law enforcement to suggest that our permit NOT be renewed at all.

Given that officers don't like filing reports or paperwork more than anyone else, chances are that if the pilot could have been found, the officer would have resolved the problem with the spectator on the spot rather than having it escalate through the filing of an official complaint, which is now on our permanent records.


Just added to the meeting agenda:
A proposal to modify FF rules by adding "Pilots must fully cooperate with all NPS law enforcement officers, including Rangers and Park Police."
