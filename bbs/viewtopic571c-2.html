New Fellow Feathers apparel

Posted: Sat Nov 22, 2008 9:21 am
by Steve Rodrigues
Chris Valley did a great job of raising money for the club in 2008 by producing and selling Fellow Feathers T-shirts. Thanks Chris!

We are going to make a new batch of shirts for 2009 and need your input and artwork. Please help us succeed by providing your feedback to the following;

Would you like hooded sweat shirts too or just T-shirts?

What colors of shirts would you like?

What kind of logo/artwork would you like on the shirt? (small logo on front, big artwork on back, no artwork on back, artwork depicting hang glider, bird, photo realistic, or simple logo, etc.)

The Fellow Feathers does not have an official logo. Question; Should we have something official that we use all the time for the pocket location and for club business, or, should we keep it informal and change it up each year?

And lastly, Can you submit any artwork for the project?

Please reply here or contact Chris (vice@flyfunstondotorg) or myself directly. (prez@flyfunstondotorg)

Thanks very much, your feedback and contributions will really help the club!
