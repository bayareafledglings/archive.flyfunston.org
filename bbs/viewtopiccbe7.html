Ed Levin Speed Gliding: Final Race Results

Posted: Mon Mar 05, 2012 3:00 pm
by crvalley
Below are final race results for Ed Levin Speed Gliding 2012.

The time (in brackets) represents a combined total of the pilot's two best times for the weekend.

Thank you to all the volunteers that helped out and the pilots who participated.

Congratulations to Brian Horgan on a new course record of 1 minute, 25 seconds!

King Post Class (Beginning with 1st Place)

1) Walter Whiteside 1:54, 1:45.5, 1:47 (3:32.5) Moyes Litesport
2) John Taylor 1:56, 1:54, 1:50, 1:47.5 (3:37.5) Wills Wing Ram Air
3) Mike Jefferson 1:50, 1:54, 1:50.5, 1:52.5 (3:40.5) Wills Wing U2
4) Jim Bo (Did Not Fly Day 1) 2:01, 1:58, (3:59) Moyes Litesport
5) Bob Shelton 2:44, 2:50 (5:34) Wills Wing U2
6) Brian Houston 2:40, (Did Not Fly Day 2) Wills Wing Sport 2
7) Terry Strahl 2:23, (Did Not Fly Day 2) Wills Wing Sport 2

Open Class (Beginning with 1st Place)

1) Brian Horgan 1:26 (DQ), 1:26.5, 1:25 (2:51.5) **New Course Record of 1:25** Aeros Combat
2) Kenny Brown 1:37, 1:33, 1:32 (3:05) Moyes Litespeed
3) Eric Froehlich 1:36, 1:40, 2:03 (3:16 (+10)=3:26) Wills Wing T2C
4) Diev Hart 1:53, 1:54, 1:49.5 (3:42.5) Moyes Litespeed
5) Wayne Michelson 1:56, 1:57.5, 2:03 (3:53.5) Icaro Laminar
6) Robert Booth 2:18, 2:11.5 (4:29.5 (+20)=4:49.5) Wills Wing T2
7) Doug Doerfler 2:17, 2:26.5, 2:42.5 (4:43.5 (+40)=5:23.5) Wills Wing T2
8 ) Henry Bittner 1:30 (Did Not Fly Day 2) Aeros Combat

Looking forward to Ed Levin Speed Gliding 2013!!!
