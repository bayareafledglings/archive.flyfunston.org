Parachute Clinic/Film Workshop- Wed, March 16th, 6:00 PM

Nova Dasalla, the internationally known, SF-based, paragliding pilot ranked #1 in the US for acrobatics will teach you how to pack your own PG/HG reserve and show you methods to ensure it opens. There's probably no one else in the country that's thrown his reserve more times using his own packing method than Nova . . . . and that's actually a good thing for this clinic! (Limited to 12 pilots for the packing clinic, unlimited for the film workshop)

Then, join Nova's discussion with Eric Blum from Photon Films on preparing for a film project.

Location: 964 Natoma,(cross street- 11th) San Fran
Time: Wed, March 16th, 6:00pm -7:30pm Reserve Packing Clinic with Nova Dasalla
7:30pm-9:30pm Film Workshop
Topic: Project Planning
- Eric Blum will present fundamentals of film making Project Planning
- Brainstorming- How to find stories that matter
- Storyboarding
- Planning for Effective Interviews
- Planning Visual Outline/shot list.

Then we will watch Nova's Infinite Stumbling and have Nova share his thoughts on the creative process, storytelling techniques, and lessons learned.

Looking forward to seeing everyone!
All times are UTC - 8 hours [ DST ]
