Fellow Feathers Glider Auction (Saturday, April 12, 2008)

Posted: Tue Mar 04, 2008 9:50 pm
by crvalley
The Fellow Feathers will hold a glider auction at Fort Funston on Saturday, April 12, 2008 (10AM to 5PM)

We will also host a small BBQ the same day....

Gliders will be available for set-up and inspection 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM...

The following gliders have been donated to Fellow Feathers and will be sold at auction:

WW Raven 229: Red / Rainbow Colors w/ Green Keel Pocket
WW Sport: Black / White / Red

The following gliders have known owners and will be sold at auction if not moved out of the clubhouse by auction time:
WW Harrier: Red / Black / Yellow (Grey Bag w/ Red “H3” Flag)
WW HPAT 146: White / Red
WW Ram Air: White
Pac Air Glider: Beige Bag w/ a Manly Pink Accent!

The following gliders have been abandoned and will be sold at auction:

Phoenix: Orange / White
WW HP or Sport: Purple / Green / White / Blue
WW HP 2: Blue / Purple / White
WW Raven 229: Blue / Purple / Orange / Yellow

Disclaimer:

All gliders are sold “as is". The Fellow Feathers of Fort Funston Hang Gliding Club makes no warranty as to a glider’s airworthiness. All gliders shall be inspected by a qualified professional before flying.

Hang Gliding Advisory: Used hang gliders should always be disassembled before flying for the first time and inspected carefully for fatigued, bent or dented down tubes, ruined bushings, bent bolts (especially the heart bolt), re-used Nyloc nuts, loose thimbles, frayed or rusted cables, tangs with non-circular holes, and on flex wings, sails badly torn or torn loose from their anchor points front and back on the keel and leading edges…
